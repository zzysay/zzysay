ZHENYU ZHANG (张振宇)

WHO AM I

I am a Ph.D. student in Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Associate Professor Tingwen Liu and Professor Li Guo. My research interests lie within the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Knowledge Graphs (KG) and Information Extraction (IE). Before I become a Ph.D. student, I received my bachelor’s degree from Central South University in 2017, advised by Professor Jianxin Wang. Up to now, I have published several papers in the world top conferences such as AAAI, WWW, IJCAI.
